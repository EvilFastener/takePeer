To take peer from wireguard you have to have administrator permission. Then go to takePeer/bin/Debug/net8.0, then
open "takePeer.exe" as administrator. In the same folder you are in, you should see new file: "Peers.json". 
Open "takePeer.exe" as administrator again to save another peer to your "Peers.json" file.

How to open "takePeer.exe" as administrator?: Right click on the file and choose "Open As Administrator" option.
If you dont have "Open As Administrator" option then just double-click on the "takePeer.exe".
